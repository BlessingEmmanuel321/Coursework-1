How to take the best pictures

With a smartphone

  1. First, start with a clean slate
  2. A little framing goes a long way
  3. Ditch the digital zoom
  4. Look for light before making your own
  5. Watch out for cloud storage shenanigans
  6. Steady yourself â€” or use a tripod
  7. Go remote with a shutter button

With a camera (professional or not)

  1. Master the Fundamentals of Composition
    • Choose a strong focal point
    • Follow the rule of thirds
    • Use leading lines
    • Put some perspective into thought
    • Frame your shot and fill the frame
  2. Make sure you have good lighting
  3. Get some lighting equipment
    • Try out a light reflector
    • Take advangtage of camera flashes and diffusers
    • Use light to create interesting effects
    • Try shooting at golden hour
  4. Learn how to edit photos like a professional
  5. Learn your camera settings!!!
